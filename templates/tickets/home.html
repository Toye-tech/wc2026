FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

Are these official FIFA tickets?

This platform provides authorized ticketing services for African fans to purchase FIFA World Cup 2026 tickets. All transactions are secured and verified through Paystack.

What currency are tickets sold in?

All tickets are priced and sold in US Dollars (USD). Paystack handles the currency conversion and accepts cards from all African banks. You'll be charged in your local currency equivalent.

How do I receive my tickets after payment?

Immediately after successful payment, you'll receive a unique ticket code and booking reference. You can retrieve your booking anytime using the "My Bookings" lookup page with your email address.

Can I buy tickets for multiple matches?

Yes! You can purchase tickets for as many matches as you like. Each booking is separate. You can buy up to 6 tickets per single booking transaction.

Is my payment information safe?

Absolutely. All payments are processed by Paystack, which is PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant — the highest standard of payment security. We never store your card details.

Do I need a visa to attend matches in the USA, Mexico or Canada?

FIFA and the host nations are working on streamlined visa processes for ticketholders. We recommend checking the official FIFA website and your country's embassy for the latest visa requirements closer to the tournament.
